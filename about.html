About.

My real name is John but people have always called me Jack. Kind of like JFK, even though his name was John everyone who really knew him called him Jack. I was born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota and after graduating high school I went to the University of North Dakota at Grand Forks. I origionally was a petroleum engineering student and was planning on trying my hand at being a drilling/production engineer in the Bakken. Oil prices crashed however and things were not looking good so I switched to Electrical Engineering with a computer science focus which turned into a double major in Electrical Engineering and computer science. After taking a few classes in computer science and doing self-study on my own I have found that I have quite a knack for programming. I am currently looking for employment as a Software Engineer. I have two specialties which are Web development and Embedded software engineering (as crazy as that may sound).
